Features

What are special in "Connect-2" is that it has 3 different playing mode:

  • Classic mode: play again the time and try to get as high score as possible.
  • Endless mode: play with levels and the target is to get as high score as possible
  • Puzzle mode: play and collect score for each levels.

The game also has 6 different image packs for you to play with: Animals, Cars, Christmas, Flags, Food and Holidays. Each image pack may be in different difficulty. Animals images seem to be the easiest, you should start playing with this image pack.
