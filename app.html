How it works
Features
  • Over 100 lovely photo frames with high definition is ready to be used.
  • Apply lovely frames to your existing photos, or apply lovely frames while taking photos. It can be used with the photos from other apps like Instagram, Camera 360, PicArts,...
  • Apply 10 photo effects (filters) and over 20 grunge textures to every photo that you have added. This helps to create the most beautiful photos that fit the lovely frames.
  • Add Text, Speech balloon and stickers
  • Share edited photo over Facebook, Twitter, Gmail,...
